    Class solver::Estimator

    Energy scale estimator.

    This estimator attempts to estimate the relevant energy range of the model it analyzes by collecting a histogram of transition costs in the random and quenched regime.

    • The random regime is sampled from random initial positions. It is used as a proxy for the high temperature regime.
    • The quenched regime is sampled from quenched states obtained by peforming several metropolis sweeps at T=0.

    From these histograms we can tune the temperature for expected boltzmann acceptance rates in either regime. This is expressed in terms of the portion of energy increasing transitions that would be accepted (transitions which don't change the energy are always accepted and the potion of energy-lowering transitions in the random regime depends on the model).
